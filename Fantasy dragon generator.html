Dragons possess immense strength and are formidable in combat, both in the air and on the ground. With razor-sharp claws, powerful jaws, and a long, agile body, they are capable of graceful maneuvers. Their most iconic ability is breathing fire, instilling fear and earning respect. Dragons are commonly associated with hoards of treasure that they fiercely guard in their lairs. Their insatiable desire for wealth includes gold, gemstones, and other valuable items, symbolizing their power and status. Known for their intelligence and wisdom, dragons hold ancient knowledge and serve as keepers of wisdom and lore. Some can speak to humans and other beings, while others communicate through telepathy or their mesmerizing gaze. Although often solitary, dragons may form alliances or hierarchies among themselves. In certain lore, they are revered as deities or rulers, commanding loyalty from lesser creatures. Legends and stories typically revolve around quests and heroic endeavors to slay or tame these mythical creatures. Dragons serve as formidable foes but also potential allies or mentors to those who prove themselves worthy.
