Dwarves are known for their long beards, varied skin tones, and hair colors ranging from fiery red to rich brown or black. They take great pride in their beards, often adorning them with intricate braids or ornaments. Dwarven fashion emphasizes durability and practicality, with rugged clothing and sturdy armor decorated with engravings and symbols representing their clans or achievements. Dwarves excel in mining, blacksmithing, and craftsmanship. They are famous for their ability to forge exceptional weapons, armor, and intricate works of art using the earth's resources. Their expertise in stonework is evident in the construction of impressive underground cities and fortresses, showcasing their architectural prowess. Characterized as steadfast and disciplined, dwarves value honor, loyalty, and community. They have a clan-based society with unique traditions, ancestral halls, and skilled craftsmen. Dwarves take great pride in their heritage and fiercely defend their people and territories. While dwarves are skilled in combat, especially in close-quarters melee, they are not typically known for their magical abilities. Nevertheless, they possess a natural resistance to magic, making them formidable opponents against spellcasters. Dwarves have a fondness for treasure and are often associated with vast underground mines filled with valuable gemstones and metals.
